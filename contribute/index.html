Fork the repo to start →

Four steps. That's it.

No experience required. If you used Claude, it counts.

1

Fork the repo

Click Fork on GitHub. You instantly get your own copy of the project to work with.

2

Create your folder

Inside author/, make a new folder with a URL-friendly name — like author/maria-from-brazil/

3

Write your page

Add one index.html file. Tell your story. Design it however you like — it's your canvas.

4

Open a PR

Submit a pull request. Automated checks run in under a minute. A maintainer reviews and merges.

Two meta tags. Required.

Paste this into the <head> of your page so the gallery can display your name and description:

<!-- Required -->
<meta name="author" content="Your Name">
<meta name="description" content="One sentence about what you built with Claude">

<!-- Optional: adds a preview image to your gallery card -->
<meta property="og:image" content="https://your-image-url.png">
All CSS and JavaScript must be inline or loaded from a public CDN — no local asset files.
Your page lives at claudeslides.com/author/your-slug/ once merged.

What belongs here

Welcome

  • A project you built or shipped with Claude's help
  • A creative experiment — writing, art, music, games
  • A tool or automation that changed your workflow
  • Something you learned that surprised you
  • A problem you couldn't solve until Claude helped
  • Beginner work — it counts just as much

Not allowed

  • Hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory content
  • Any sexualization or harm targeting minors
  • Harassment or personal attacks
  • Malicious code, scripts, or injection attacks
  • Spam, scams, or disinformation
